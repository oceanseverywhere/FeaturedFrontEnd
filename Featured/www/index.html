What is Featured ?

Featured is a new mobile app that allows restaurants to connect directly with you to feature their specials and live deals while collecting your feedback. It offers you a single access point for information to help you decide where to eat, drink, and be merry. Featured also gives suggestions for where to go based on past experiences and your mood. You can also choose to receive push notifications from your favorite restaurants. Whether your looking for food or drinks, or you're selling them, Featured is the only app you need.
